Ingredients:
 - 1 pack Shin Ramyun
 - 2 eggs

 Directions:
  1. Boil water in a pot.  Add in flavoring package.

  2. Put the uncooked ramyun into the pot.  Constantly push the floating ramyun
     down until it starts to naturally separate apart.

  3. After separation, add in the two eggs.  Turn off the heat, cover the pot
     with a lid, and wait ~3 minutes.

  4. Enjoy.
